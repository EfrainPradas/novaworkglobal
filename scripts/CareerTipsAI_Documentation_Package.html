Executive Summary


CareerTipsAI is an AI-powered career transformation platform that combines 65+ years of combined C-suite coaching experience with advanced artificial intelligence to accelerate professional growth.


Key Differentiators

  • **Agentic AI™**: Autonomous career agent that thinks, plans, executes, and adapts
  • **Warm Intelligence**: AI with human touch - not a chatbot, but a true professional partner
  • **Weekly Reinvention Cycles**: Structured Monday planning + Friday reflection loops
  • **Human + AI Hybrid**: Scale coaching expertise through technology

    • Target Launch

    • **MVP Timeline**: 16 weeks (4 months)
    • **Initial Market**: English-speaking professionals (US, UK, Canada, Australia)
    • **Post-MVP**: Expand to ES, PT, FR, IT languages
