The 5-step strategic search framework

Each step supports the next. Together they transform job search into an intentional operating system rather than a reactive routine.

Step
01

Strategy & Targeting

Start with rigorous market research. Clarify what you want, identify growing sectors, map roles to your skills and ambitions, and build conviction before outreach begins.

Myth to replace “I’ll know the right job when I see it.”
Reality to adopt Determine what you are looking for before sending a single application.
Step
02

Optimize Materials

Treat online applications as one optimized tool inside the larger strategy. Resume quality, ATS alignment, and role-specific tailoring directly affect whether a human ever sees your profile.

ATS optimization Structure resumes for applicant tracking systems without losing clarity or credibility.
Strategic tailoring Align experience, keywords, and value narrative to the actual requirements of the role.
Step
03

Automate & Apply with Intent

Use technology to reduce repetitive search work and surface higher-alignment opportunities. Automation should support discernment—not replace it.

AI-powered ranking Scan the market and prioritize roles by true fit instead of endless scrolling.
Professional follow-up Stay visible with concise, timely follow-up that reinforces seriousness without becoming noise.
Step
04

Build Relationships

Many of the best jobs never reach public boards. This step focuses on recruiters, informational conversations, referrals, and long-term relationship capital.

Hidden hiring pipeline Understand how agencies, retained firms, and contingency recruiters operate so you can communicate value efficiently.
Authentic networking Build mutually beneficial relationships that make your name come to mind when opportunities appear.
Step
05

Strengthen Online Presence

Before recruiters call, they search. An optimized profile, thoughtful portfolio, and visible professional brand help you become not only discoverable, but compelling.

Recruiter discoverability Surface in relevant searches 24/7 through a polished LinkedIn and aligned keyword strategy.
Credibility signal Expand your footprint with portfolio assets, proof of work, and thought leadership content.
